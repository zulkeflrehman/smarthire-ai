How to Use SmartHire AI

1. Post a New Job

Go to New Job under the Actions section. Fill in the job title, company, location, and a detailed description. A thorough description results in more accurate keyword matches.

2. Upload Candidate Resumes

Navigate to your job details page. Drag-and-drop or select candidate resumes in PDF format. You can upload multiple resumes at once to screen them concurrently.

3. Screen Candidates with ML

Click the Start Screening button. The system processes resumes using our advanced machine learning pipeline, featuring BERT Semantic Analysis, TF-IDF Term Relevance, and Skill Keyword Mapping.

4. View Analysis & Rankings

Review the ranks, overall match scores, and BERT/TF-IDF weight distributions. Explore matched and unmatched skills for each candidate, and update their status (Shortlisted, Rejected, or Pending) on the fly.
